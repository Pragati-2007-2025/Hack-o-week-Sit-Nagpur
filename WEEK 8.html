About

Chasing light around the world

For the past decade, I've wandered through mountains, forests, deserts, and coastlines, seeking those fleeting moments when light transforms ordinary landscapes into something magical.

My work has been featured in National Geographic, Outdoor Photography Magazine, and exhibited in galleries across Europe and North America.
